National State & District War Room Task Force

Battling COVID19 on the ground

How can we Help

Our organisation works with governments, institutions and communities. We want to work with each faction in such a way that is beneficial to them in achieving their goals from spreading awareness in communities, sourcing equipment and expert advice to governments, to sharing educational training material to institutions. Our Taskforce was built so that we may utilize our limited resources of time and man power to maximize our outputs. The Taskforce helps us keep track of the consequential things that must be done to ease the pressure our governmental leaders and health care professional are facing every hour of every day.

Pan-India Matching

We at CovidIndia.org aim to help alleviate the disbalance between the supply and demand for PPE. We are working on ways to meet the demand for PPE and quality control processes for additive manufacturing of PPE. We are also focused on specifications and manufacturing ramp-up for ventilators, oxygen concentrators, rapid-detection kits, and other equipment.

Scaling Testing in India

We provide information to the public about the current situation and preparedness of the government in terms of testing and treatment. We have an interactive map that lets Indians find their national, state test centres and private labs, helplines and many other information essential to their lives in this situation. We also share resources on the procedure of procuring tests.

Data Based research to give reports to Govt

We prepare Cibil-type Bureau for count data (user location sharing, ambulance trips etc, etc) - for predictive modelling and marking emerging hotspots of risk. We are coordinating with on-ground partners and govt teams to aid in the transport and delivery of essential goods along with logistic support in test kit imports.

Connect CSR fund to Hospitals and folks

Apart from sharing educational training materials for healthcare workers and corporate institutions, we are also working with hospitals and doctors in need to connect them with much needed PPEs and funds.

Connect war rooms to experts across the industry

During this time of national crisis, we have joined forces with the states war rooms of 6+ states pan-India and helping them connect with the authorities in various fields of healthcare, manufacturing and economics to suggest the best possible methods to help the millions of citizens and those on the frontlines fighting this pandemic.

Manage State/National War rooms

In addition to connecting war rooms with the right personnel, we are also managing the demand supply of PPE, helping in curation of best possible medical advice and in the tech support to create a harmonious and smooth flow of work and information in the various channels of communication.

Who are We

At CovidIndia.org, we are an action oriented group of people who value impact above all else. With our Taskforce we want all our lines of work to have maximum impact. With numbers of infected people steadily rising, not only in India but across the world, it is integral that our work is quick, decisive and impactful. This is why our team dedicatedly follows our Taskforce which dictates the many number of important things we do in this ever changing dynamic, so that we always have direction and set goals toward which we put in our energies.
